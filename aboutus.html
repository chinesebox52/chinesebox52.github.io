Welcome to Chinese Box Courtyard Beijing!

We are located in a Hutong called, Xi Si Bei Er Tiao. Hutong means a narrow alleyway, often positioned like a maze. Before development took place in Beijing, Hutongs used to be everywhere. Not to worry, there are still plenty of Hutongs around Beijing, which are the best choice if you want to just walk around, get lost, while experiencing the Real Beijing and its people, taking a walk, talking with the neighbours.
